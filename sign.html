Our History

Space Walkers was founded on the hope of human beings becoming a multi-planitary civilization. Thanks to the advancement of microchips and renewable energy, we were able to get start operations in January of 2050!

After getting the beta period of roaming through planets with our volunteers, we were able to open the opportuniy to travel to 2 different planets and the moon.

We take great pride in making sure our passengers are safe and entertaine throughout their entire experience with us. From booking to landing on Earth with our Mars-themed dinner.
