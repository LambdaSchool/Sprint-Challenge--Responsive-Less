Aging in Place has Never
Been More Important

This is not a small problem

  • Today 50,000,000 million Americans are over 65
  • By 2030 the worldwide population over 65 will be 1,000,000,000 (one billion)
  • 15% of the population over 50 in Europe are informal caregivers


The financial impact is staggering

  • In 2014 Medicaid long term care costs were $152 Billion
  • By 2024 long term care is expected to cost $1.1 Trillion dollars globally
  • 65% of assisted living revenues come from patients or their families
