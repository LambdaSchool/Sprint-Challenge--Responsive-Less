A little bit about Spacewalkers...

Nasa looked at the large rocket in his hands and felt happy. He walked over to the window and reflected on his quiet surroundings. He had always loved cold Space with its old-fashioned, open oceans. It was a place that encouraged his tendency to feel happy.

Then he saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Mars . Mars was a remarkable giant with dry legs and enormous hair. Nasa gulped. He glanced at his own reflection.

He was a nice, honest, tea drinker with huge legs and big hair. His friends saw him as a hot, huge hero. Once, he had even helped a striped child recover from a flying accident. But not even a nice person who had once helped a striped child recover from a flying accident, was prepared for what Mars had in store today.

The sun shone like rocket launches, making Nasa peaceful. As Nasa stepped outside and Mars came closer, he could see the tasteless glint in his eye. Mars gazed with the affection of 4851 brave adorable aliens. Then Mars came inside for a nice cup of tea.
