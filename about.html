About SpaceWalkers

Here at SpaceWalkers, we bring the universe to you.

What does that mean?

We are a youth camp that focuses on teaching the next generation about Space, Planetary Bodies, and The Future of NASA and how they can become the next Neil Armstrong!

Summer Camps

Learn what it's actually like to be in space!

At our summer camps, young aspiring astronauts will experience 0 gravity in our sense deprivation tanks and feel what its actually like to take off with our centrifuge simulators (actual g-forces reached will not exceed 3 for safety reasons)
